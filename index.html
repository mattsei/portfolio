I am Sei

I am an Australian Designer, Creator, Audio Engineer, and Junior Full-Stack Developer.
Whilst a relative newcomer to the world of programming, I have an extensive background in design and fabrication and am embracing development as a means to build upon my current skillset and experience, and create truly immersive and satisfying user experiences.
I am also the founder of BIOS Collective, an arts & media collective that promotes creators, artists, photographers, and musicians of the cyberpunk persuasion.
This page serves as a showcase of some of the works I have created and been involved in over recent years.
